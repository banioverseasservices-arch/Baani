FAQs

Do you charge for counselling?

Our initial counselling session is free. We offer various service packages with transparent pricing. Contact us for details regarding specialized services.

Which Australian universities do you work with?

We help students apply to all major Australian institutions including Go8 universities, UTS, RMIT, and other prestigious universities across Australia.

Can you help with visas?

Yes, we provide complete visa guidance including document preparation, application assistance, and interview coaching to maximize your visa approval chances.

What is the typical timeline for university admission?

The process typically takes 2-4 months from initial consultation to admission letter. Visa processing usually takes 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you provide support after I reach Australia?

Absolutely! We provide post-arrival support including accommodation assistance, orientation, banking setup, and ongoing guidance throughout your studies.

What if my application gets rejected?

We provide comprehensive support if your application is rejected. We'll analyze the reason, suggest improvements, and help with reapplication to other universities.
